School of Mathematics and Statistics • The University of Melbourne

I am a PhD student focused on modelling diseases and invasive pests to support decision making.

I am interested in how we build models to understand the dynamics of disease and invasive species spread while also ensuring our modelling is explainable and justifiable for decision making. My PhD has a focus on modelling testing strategies, in particular understanding how delayed detection can ultimately impact the spread and management of a disease or invasive species outbreak.

When I'm not working on my PhD, you can usually find me knitting or reading in a comfy chair or out on a hike with my partner.
